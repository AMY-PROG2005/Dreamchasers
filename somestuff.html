WHAT TO EXPECT ONBOARD

Asteroids are small, rocky objects that orbit the Sun. Most of them can be found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. Asteroids are believed to be remnants from the early formation of the solar system. They come in a wide range of sizes, from tiny grains of dust to objects hundreds of kilometers across. Asteroids are made up of various materials, including metals, silicate rocks, and carbon-rich materials. Studying asteroids can provide valuable insights into the formation and evolution of our solar system. Some asteroids have even been visited by spacecraft, allowing scientists to collect detailed data about their composition and structure
